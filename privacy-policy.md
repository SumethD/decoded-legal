# Decoded — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 13, 2026

Decoded ("we", "us", "our") operates the Decoded mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account information:** Email address (via Clerk authentication).
- **Scan history:** Barcodes scanned, timestamps, and results viewed.
- **Community submissions:** Product photos (front-of-pack, ingredients label, and optional nutrition label) and OCR-derived ingredient text uploaded through the submission feature.
- **Camera access:** The App requests camera permission solely to scan barcodes and capture label photos when you submit a product. Camera images are captured only when you actively take a photo and are never accessed in the background.
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, app version, locale, and (on Android) the Android Advertising ID (AAID) for rewarded ads.
- **Usage analytics:** Anonymized event data (scans, submissions, feature usage) via PostHog.
- **Crash diagnostics:** Crash reports and performance traces via Sentry, used to identify and fix app errors. Crash reports may include device model, OS version, app version, and stack traces.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Ingredient lookup:** Matching scanned barcodes to our product database.
- **Improving our database:** Using submitted label photos and OCR data to expand product coverage. Approved submissions may be shared with Open Food Facts under their open data licence.
- **Analytics:** Understanding app usage patterns to improve the experience.
- **Crash debugging:** Diagnosing and resolving technical issues.
- **Advertising (Sri Lanka):** Google AdMob serves rewarded video ads that grant additional daily scans. See Section 5 for details.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

We store data with the following providers. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

- **Backend:** Convex (US-hosted, SOC 2 Type II compliant)
- **Authentication:** Clerk (US-hosted, SOC 2 compliant)
- **Analytics:** PostHog (US / EU-hosted)
- **Crash diagnostics:** Sentry (US / EU-hosted)

**Retention periods:**

- **Account, scan history, and submissions:** retained for as long as your account is active.
- **Anonymized analytics events:** retained for up to 12 months.
- **Crash diagnostics:** retained for up to 90 days.
- **Upon account deletion (Section 6):** all account data is permanently removed from our live systems immediately. Encrypted backups containing this data are overwritten within 30 days.

## 5. Advertising and the Android Advertising ID

In Sri Lanka, the App displays rewarded video ads through Google AdMob. Watching a rewarded ad grants you additional daily scans. To serve these ads, Google AdMob collects:

- **Android Advertising ID (AAID)** — a resettable identifier that lets ad networks measure ad performance and (where applicable) deliver personalized ads.
- **Coarse device and network information** — device type, OS version, IP address, and approximate location derived from your IP address.

By default, ads in the App may be personalized based on your interests. You can change this at any time:

- **Reset or delete your AAID:** open your Android device's **Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID** (or **Delete advertising ID**).
- **Opt out of personalized ads:** open your Android device's **Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**.

Google's handling of ad data is further described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and the [AdMob Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9740416).

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

- **Access:** You may request a copy of the data we hold about you by emailing the contact in Section 10.
- **Deletion:** You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from inside the App at any time: **Profile → Delete Account**. Deletion removes your Decoded profile, scan history, submissions, uploaded label photos, saved settings, and your authentication record within 24 hours and cannot be undone.
- **Export:** You may request a machine-readable export of your data by emailing the contact in Section 10.
- **Opt-out of personalized ads:** see Section 5.
- **Opt-out of analytics:** a toggle to disable analytics is planned in a future release; until then you may request analytics suppression by email.

## 7. PDPA Compliance (Sri Lanka)

We comply with Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022 (PDPA). Your personal data is processed lawfully, with your consent, and for the legitimate purposes described in this Policy. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account in-app or by contacting us.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Decoded is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it.
