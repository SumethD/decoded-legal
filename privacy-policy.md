# Decoded — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 30, 2026

Decoded ("we", "us", "our") operates the Decoded mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

- **Account information:** Email address (via Clerk authentication)
- **Scan history:** Barcodes scanned, timestamps, and results viewed
- **Submitted photos:** Ingredient label images uploaded through the community submission feature
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system, app version, and locale
- **Usage analytics:** Anonymized event data (scans, submissions, feature usage) via PostHog

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- **Ingredient lookup:** Matching scanned barcodes to our product database
- **Improving our database:** Using submitted label photos and OCR data to expand product coverage
- **Analytics:** Understanding app usage patterns to improve the experience
- **Ads (Sri Lanka):** Google AdMob may serve rewarded video ads. AdMob collects device identifiers per Google's privacy policy.

## 3. Data Storage

- **Backend:** Convex (US-hosted, SOC 2 Type II compliant)
- **Authentication:** Clerk (US-hosted, SOC 2 compliant)
- **Analytics:** PostHog (US/EU-hosted)
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest.

## 5. Your Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data by emailing us
- **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion (available in a future update, or by contacting us)
- **Export:** Request data export in a machine-readable format
- **Opt-out:** Disable analytics tracking in app settings (coming soon)

## 6. PDPA Compliance (Sri Lanka)

We comply with Sri Lanka's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA). Your personal data is processed lawfully, with your consent, and for legitimate purposes. You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting us.

## 7. Health Disclaimer

Decoded provides ingredient information for educational purposes only. It is not a substitute for medical, dietary, or professional health advice. Always consult a qualified professional before making health decisions based on ingredient information.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Decoded is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated date.
